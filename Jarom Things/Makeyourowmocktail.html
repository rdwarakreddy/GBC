What to Expect

Step into a one-of-a-kind flavor experience at Green Bean Coffee, where creativity meets taste. This interactive event invites you to explore the art of crafting your own personalized beverage from start to finish.

Choose your base flavor, experiment with modifiers and accents, and build a drink that truly reflects your preferences. Whether you enjoy bold, sweet, or refreshing combinations, the possibilities are endless.

Enhance your creation by selecting your favorite toppings and even the style of glass you’d like your drink served in. Every detail is in your hands, making each drink unique and memorable

Our expert mixologists will be there to guide you through the process, sharing tips and techniques to help you master the art of mixing flavors like a pro.
